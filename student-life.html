A Home Away From Home

Boarding & Facilities

Our student residences are designed to provide a safe, comfortable, and disciplined environment. We separate dormitories for boys and girls, each managed by dedicated resident wardens and matrons.
  • Spacious and well-ventilated dormitories
  • 24/7 Security and surveillance
  • Clean treated water and sanitation facilities
  • Study halls for evening prep

Nourishing Body & Mind

Health & Nutrition

We prioritize a balanced diet to keep our students energetic and healthy. Our menu is carefully planned to provide all essential nutrients.
  • Balanced meals (Posho, Rice, Matooke, etc.)
  • Purified and safe drinking water
  • Special meals for celebratory events

  • Head Prefect (Male & Female)
  • Academic & Library Prefects
  • Sports & Entertainment Prefects
  • Chapel & Spiritual Leaders
