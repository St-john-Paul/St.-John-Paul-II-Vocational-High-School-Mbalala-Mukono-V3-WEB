## Getting Started

### Accessing the System
1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to `http://localhost:5173` (or your deployment URL)
3. Click **"Login"** in the navigation menu
4. Select your role (Admin, Staff, or Student)

### First-Time Login
Use the credentials provided by your administrator. You will be able to change your password after logging in.

## Admin Guide

### Dashboard Overview
After logging in, you'll see:
- **Total Students** - Current enrollment count
- **Total Staff** - Number of teachers and staff
- **Outstanding Fees** - Financial summary

### 1. Managing Staff Allocations

**Purpose:** Assign teachers to specific classes and subjects

**Steps:**
1. Click **"Allocations"** in the sidebar
2. Select a class from the dropdown (e.g., "Senior One (S.1)")
3. You'll see all subjects for that class
4. For each subject:
   - Click **"Assign"** if no teacher is assigned
   - Click **"Change"** to reassign to a different teacher
5. Select a teacher from the dropdown
6. Click **"Save Assignment"**

**Tips:**
- Use "Vocational Class" view to assign instructors to vocational subjects
- The system prevents duplicate assignments
- Changes appear instantly on teacher dashboards

### 2. Managing Curriculum

**Purpose:** Add, edit, or remove subjects

**Steps:**
1. Go to **Admin → Academics**
2. Use the **tabs** to filter subjects:
   - **All Subjects** - View everything
   - **O-Level** - Secondary subjects (S.1-S.4)
   - **A-Level** - Advanced subjects (S.5-S.6)
   - **Vocational** - Practical courses
3. To **Add** a subject:
   - Click **"Add Subject"** (top right)
   - Fill in Name, Code, Category, and Core status
   - Click **"Save"**
4. To **Edit** a subject:
   - Click the **pencil icon** next to the subject
   - Modify fields
   - Click **"Save"**
5. To **Delete** a subject:
   - Click the **trash icon**
   - Confirm deletion

### 3. Managing Students

**Steps:**
1. Go to **Admin → Students**
2. Click **"Add Student"**
3. Fill in:
   - Full Name
   - Registration Number
   - Class Level (e.g., S.1)
   - Stream (optional)
   - Parent contact info
4. The system auto-creates a login account
5. Default password: `password123`

### 4. Managing Staff

**Steps:**
1. Go to **Admin → Staff**
2. Click **"Add Staff"**
3. Enter:
   - Full Name
   - Username (for login)
   - Role/Position (e.g., "Mathematics Teacher")
   - Email and Phone
4. Default password: `staff123`
5. Teachers can update their own contact info later

## Staff Guide

### Dashboard Overview
Your dashboard shows:
- **Assigned Classes** - Number of classes you teach
- **Total Students** - Students in your classes
- **Subjects Taught** - Your teaching load

### 1. Viewing Your Profile

**Steps:**
1. Click **"Settings"** in the sidebar
2. Go to **"My Profile"** tab
3. You can see:
   - Your full name (read-only)
   - Your role (read-only)
   - Email address (**editable**)
   - Phone number (**editable**)

**To Update:**
1. Edit Email or Phone fields
2. Click **"Save Changes"**

### 2. Changing Your Password

**Steps:**
1. Click **"Settings"** in the sidebar
2. Go to **"Security"** tab
3. Enter:
   - Current Password
   - New Password
4. Click **"Update Password"**

### 3. Managing Notifications

**Steps:**
1. Click **"Settings"** in the sidebar
2. Go to **"Notifications"** tab
3. Toggle preferences:
   - Email alerts
   - SMS notifications
4. Click **"Save Preferences"**

### 4. Entering Student Marks

**Steps:**
1. Go to **Staff → Enter Marks**
2. Select a class from your assigned classes
3. Choose the subject
4. Enter marks for each student
5. Click **"Submit"**

### 5. Viewing Your Timetable

**Steps:**
1. Go to **Staff → Timetable**
2. Your schedule appears automatically
3. You can see:
   - Daily schedule
   - Class assignments
   - Break times

## Student Guide

### Dashboard Overview
After login, you'll see:
- Your profile information
- Class and stream
- Current academic standing

### 1. Viewing Results

**Steps:**
1. Go to **Student → Results**
2. Select a term
3. View your marks by subject
4. Download report card (if available)

### 2. Checking Fee Status

**Steps:**
1. Go to **Student → Finance**
2. View:
   - Total fees required
   - Amount paid
   - Outstanding balance
3. See payment history

### 3. Viewing Timetable

**Steps:**
1. Go to **Student → Timetable**
2. Your class timetable loads automatically
3. View:
   - Daily schedule
   - Subject teachers
   - Break times

### 4. Browsing Curriculum

**Steps:**
1. Go to **Student → Curriculum**
2. View subjects for:
   - O-Level
   - A-Level (if applicable)
   - Vocational courses
3. See which subjects are core vs. elective

## Common Issues & Solutions

### "I forgot my password"
**Solution:** Contact the school administrator to reset it.

### "My dashboard shows wrong information"
**Solution:** 
1. Refresh the page (F5)
2. Log out and log back in
3. If persistent, contact IT support

### "I can't see my classes" (Staff)
**Solution:** Ask the administrator to assign you to classes via **Admin → Allocations**.

### "Subjects not appearing in Curriculum"
**Solution:** Administrator needs to add them via **Admin → Academics**.
